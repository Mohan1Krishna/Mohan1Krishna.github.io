Hyderabad Pharma City


Envisioned as a Smart, Innovative, Competitive, Eco-sensitive & Sustainable integrated industrial cluster for the entire pharma value chain:


  • Improve India’s health security through end-to-end affordable & innovative products
  • Promote competitive domestic manufacturing in line with Make-in-India initiative
  • Provide plug-n-play infra facilities of international benchmark
  • Serve as a one-stop-destination for global companies
  • Dedicated zones for Pharma Manufacturing, Pharma R&D and high innovation, Pharma University and residential cluster, among others

Medical Devices Park

Medical Devices Park, launched in 2017, has become the destination-of-choice for leading medical devices companies in the Country. Spread across 276 Acres in Sultanpur village of Sangareddy district, the park offers dedicated ecosystem for Medtech innovation and manufacturing. About 16 companies are in the process of setting up their R&D / Manufacturing facilities in the park including the Asia’s largest stent manufacturing facility for manufacture of Coronary Stents & Balloon Catheters with a proposed investment of Rs 250-crore by Sahajanand Medical Technologies.

Genome Valley

Genome Valley is India’s largest and first organized cluster for lie sciences R&D activities. Emerging as the preferred destination for major Indian and global life sciences companies.It attracts a healthy mix of companies belonging to Agri-biotech, CRM, Pharmaceuticals, Biopharma, Vaccine space and Medical Devices space.
